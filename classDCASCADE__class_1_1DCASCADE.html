◆ passing_cascade_in_outputs

DCASCADE_class.DCASCADE.passing_cascade_in_outputs

---—Step 1 : Cascades generated from the reaches upstream during the present time step, are passing the inlet of the reach (stored in Qbi_pass[n]).

This computational step make them pass to the outlet of the reach or stop in the reach, depending if their velocity make them arrive at the outlet before the end of the time step or not.
