Detailed Description

Sediment cascade object.

A sediment cascade is a volume mobilised from a reach during one time step.

Parameters
provenanceReach from which the cascade is mobilised at this time step
elapsed_timeTime since the begining of the time step. Is updated as the cascade moves through different reaches.
volumeThe mobilised sediment volume itself. It is a 2d array. N_col = n_classes + metadata. N_row = number of layers separated by initial provenance
